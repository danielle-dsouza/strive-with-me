Our Story
Behind Strive with ME

A grassroots initiative focused on helping young adults and their families.

Maria Le spent many years searching for services to help her eldest daughter who is diagnosed with autism. Unfortuntely, she realized many services were either too far or not suitable. She was disappointed that there was a lack of programs for those on the Spectrum who are transitioning from highschool to post-secondary and/or employment.

Her youngest daughter has been dealing with depression and anxiety for several years. Although her daughter plays team sports and is in the mainstream school program, Maria noticed she, too, struggled with the sociatal stigmatization of Mental Health.

Maria decided she wanted to help, not just her daughters, but the many other young adults who face such difficulties.

Our Mission
You make your goals.
We help you acheive them.
Strive With ME is a non-profit organization seeking to encourage young adults with autism to improve social skills through recreational activities! With the help of our tireless staff, we organize fundraisers, community-building events, and in-depth training sessions for our volunteers. We are committed to helping each and every person reach their unique goals!
