Our Story
Strive with ME Backstory

A grassroots initiative focused on helping young adults and their families.

Maria Le spent many years searching for services to help her eldest daughter, diagnosed with autism. Unfortuntely, she realized many services were either out of area or were not suitable. She was disappointed that there seemed to be a lack of programs for those who are on the Spectrum and are transitioning from highschool to post-secondary and/or employment.

Her youngest daughter has been dealing with depression and anxiety for several years. Although she plays team sports and is in the mainstream school program, Maria noticed her daughter struggled with the sociatal stigmatization of Mental Health.

She decided she wanted to help not just her daughters, but the many other young adults who face such difficulties, as well.

Our Mission
You make your goals.
We help you acheive them.
A grassroots initiative focused on helping teens and young adults with High-Functioning Autism and their families. Strive With ME is a non-profit organization seeking to encourage help young adults with autism develop social skills through recreational activities! With the help of our tireless staff, we organize fundraisers, community-building events, and in-depth training sessions for our volunteers. We are committed to helping each and every person reach their unique goals!
